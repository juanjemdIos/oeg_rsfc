FRSM-12 metric: Does the software provide references to other objects that support its use?

Determining the usefulness of a piece of software is often aided by understanding what it is used with.

Source: This metric is an instance of FSRM-12, developed within the FAIR-IMPACT project. More information about the project is available at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15535629
